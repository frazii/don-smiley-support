FAQ

I have a suggestion or found a bug

We'd love to hear it. Send us a note with the details.

The game crashed or won't open

Force-close and reopen first. If that doesn't help, delete and reinstall. Your high score is saved to Game Center. Still stuck? Email us.

How do I see the global leaderboard?

Sign into Game Center in Settings → Game Center. The Leaderboard button will appear on the main menu and score screen once you're signed in.
