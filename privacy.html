Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

Your Privacy

We don't collect your personal data

Don Smiley Run does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The developer (Fraz Anjum) has no access to who you are, where you are, or how you play.

No analytics or advertising SDKs

The game contains no third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or tracking SDKs of any kind. Nothing in the app monitors your behavior or device.

Game Center (leaderboard)

Don Smiley Run uses Apple Game Center to power the global leaderboard. If you sign into Game Center, Apple handles your account data under Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer never receives your Game Center identity or personal details.

In-app purchases (tip jar)

Optional tips are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit and the App Store. The developer does not handle payment information. Purchase records are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data stored on your device

The game saves your high score and settings locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Children's privacy

Don Smiley Run does not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. Because no data is collected at all, the game is safe for players of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If anything here changes, an updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. As long as no data is collected, this policy is unlikely to change.
